# Dither — itch.io page copy

## Tagline

The crank is your steering wheel: 1-bit top-down circuit racing on Playdate.

## Description

**Dither** is a collection of original 1-bit pixel/sprite games for the
Playdate, and it opens with **Sprint** — a top-down circuit racer in the
spirit of the arcade classics. Eight single-screen tracks, three relentless
drone cars, and one perfect control gimmick: the crank is literally your
steering wheel. Hold A for throttle, feather B for the brakes, and thread
the hairpins with your wrist.

Every track keeps its own best-lap record on your device, so the real race
starts after you've won: the drones run flawless lines at fixed speeds, but
you're faster — if you can stay off the walls. Solid-white car, hollow-white
rivals, dithered grandstands: pure high-contrast 1-bit, built for the
Playdate screen.

## Features

- Crank-as-steering-wheel driving — direct, analog, and all yours
  (d-pad fallback included)
- 8 circuits, from easy ovals to wall-lined hairpin nests
- 3 drone rivals on inside / centre / outside racing lines
- 3-lap or 5-lap races, skippable countdown
- Per-track best-lap records saved on the device
- Chunky 16-angle car sprites converted from CC0 artwork; all-synth
  sound effects

## Controls

Crank = steer · A = throttle / start · B = brake & reverse ·
D-pad = full-lock steering, plus track & lap select on the title screen

## Installing (no dev tools needed)

Download `Sprint.pdx.zip` from Releases (or `dist/`), then either:

- **On a Playdate**: upload the zip at
  https://play.date/account/sideload/ and download it to the device from
  Settings → Games, or
- **In the Playdate Simulator** (free with the Playdate SDK): unzip and
  open the `.pdx` in the Simulator.

The full player's manual ships in the repo as `MANUAL.md`.
